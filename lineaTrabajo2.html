Line of work

Cítricos Selectos San Martín

A planter and trader of Persian limes with experience of more than 13 years. The company has successfully realized foreign sales with customers in the United States, Korea, Japan, France, Germany, Holland, Canada and Poland. In addition it has 2 certifications of SAGARPA SENASICA and U.S. FDA Food Safety Modernization.

Abonat

A products and services company which mission is the development of the farmland treatment by leaching and the correct input advice, according to soil characteristics and type of production, they can improve agronomic levels using uniquely natural and organic processes.

Daylight intelligence

Company specialized in Information Technologies. Which focuses on the generation of data to companies, in this way are supported with information for decision-making and generate a better production.

Enercotec

It´s a company specialized in alternative energies using technology of transition to the use of new fuels that are not of fossil base, developing new technologies like new alternatives to the oil as fuel, that allow to be applied in the current technologies industrial and automotive world-wide to optimize combustion and reduce the consumption of fossil fuels through the use of hydrogen without the need for significant modifications, contributing in a significant way to the abatement of the polluting emissions and greenhouse effect produced by the carbon content of our traditional fuels.

Rico Lunch

It is a Mexican company dedicated to the development of 100% natural products with high nutritional content, with an experience of more than 10 years in the elaboration of dehydrated food, a great advantage is that their products do not contain any artificial flavor and/or conservative , they are also incredibly easy to prepare.

Nelly cently

it´s a company based on production of native ancestral maize, unlike traditional white maize, the ancestral maize are rich in vitamins, antioxidants and fibers, natural properties that have been preserved over the years and give as result the flavor, color and texture that makes them unique in the market transformed by Mexican hands of our native of Tuxpan and the mountain range of Manantlán to the south of Jalisco,the way they work the soil is a traditional and 100% natural way without chemicals and fertilizers . the different products that are offered are tortillas, totopos and tostadas..
